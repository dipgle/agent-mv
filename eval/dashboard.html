About compliance checks

Two layers run on every produced video before publish:

  • Content moderation: NSFW (NudeNet), real-person face (OpenCV), trademark similarity (CLIP), voice-clone consent check. Critical → auto-reject. Major → flagged for human review.
  • C2PA Content Credentials: embeds AI-disclosure manifest per EU AI Act Article 50 + platform requirements (Meta, TikTok, YouTube). Signed if C2PA_SIGNING_KEY_PATH env var is set; unsigned Annotated Credentials otherwise.

See docs/c2pa.md for full setup instructions.
